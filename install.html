Install CinéMath

Two paths: tap the magic link your admin sent (phone) or sideload the APK (TV).

Phone Install via magic link

  1. Open the magic invite link your admin sent (looks like bicyclecrasher.github.io/WatchTrack/?invite=…).
  2. Tap Install when your browser prompts to add CinéMath to your Home Screen.
  3. Complete the in-app setup: pick a display name, then (optionally) connect Trakt.

That's it. The Home Screen icon launches CinéMath as a standalone app.

Android TV / Fire TV Sideload the APK

Sony Bravia Google TV and Fire TV don't accept Home Screen installs. Use the signed APK instead.

  1. On your TV: Settings → Security & Restrictions → Unknown Sources, toggle on for your TV's web browser or file manager.
  2. Open the TV's web browser, navigate to bicyclecrasher.github.io/WatchTrack/install.apk. The browser downloads the latest signed APK from GitHub Releases.
  3. Open the downloaded APK and tap Install.
  4. Launch CinéMath from the TV's app drawer. The "Pair this device" screen appears with a QR.
  5. On your already-paired phone, scan the QR. Confirm in the popup. The TV reloads as your account.

Replacing a lost device

If you've lost access to your old phone, ask your admin for a reconnect link. Open it on a new device, confirm "replacing my old phone," and your watchlist + ratings come right back.
